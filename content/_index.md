## About {#about}

I am Daisuke Nohara, a B4 undergraduate student at the Institute of Science Tokyo, working in the Rio Yokota Lab. My research focuses on RL for improving LLM reasoning.

## Research Interests {#research-interests}

- Reasoning in large language models
- Reinforcement learning for LLMs
- Efficient inference and length control
